Award360° 100 Poster +D: Yui Takada CL: Design 360° 2021
This poster was designed for the “Award360° 100” design competition submission. Centered on the circular ring symbol of the DSIGN360° logo, various lines are used to express diversity and individuality, which are then brought together into a circle to represent an attitude of “inclusiveness.” The design reflects the idea that design thinking can help create a positive cycle in society, and this concept guided the creation of the poster.
