Takada Yui is a graphic designer, art director, AGI member, and professor at Tokyo Zokei University.

Born in Tokyo in 1980, he graduated from the Kuwasawa Design School.

He is a director of “Allright,” a company composed of the graphic design division “Allright Graphics,” the letterpress printing division “Allright Printing,” and the music label “Allright Music.”

His awards include the JAGDA New Designer Award in 2011, the Japan Package Design Awards Gold Prize and the Kuwasawa Award in 2015, the Tokyo ADC Award in 2019, and the TDC Award in 2020.
